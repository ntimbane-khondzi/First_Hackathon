About Me

My name is Khondzi Ntimbane, I'm 24 years old, originally from Pienaar but raised in Lekazi, a township near Nelspruit in the Mpumalanga province. I recently completed my tertiary education with a degree in Financial Planning. During my studies, I became aware of how widespread financial illiteracy is in many of our communities — often due to limited access to quality financial education. That realization inspired me to focus my academic work on practical solutions.

One of my key projects involved conceptualizing a financial management tool — a software idea that would not only help users track and manage their expenses but also offer accessible financial advice on investing and protecting assets, all integrated within everyday banking apps. My goal is to contribute to financial empowerment by making smart financial tools available and easy to use, especially for underserved communities.
